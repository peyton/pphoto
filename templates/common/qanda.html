Questions & Answers

How long does it take to get my photos?
Turnaround time is usually two to three days from when we receive your merchandise.
How do I receive the final images?
Any way you’d like. Typically, customers download their images on the web, or we can ship a CD overnight.
Some of my products require assembly. Can you do this?
Of course! We can assemble anything. Product assembly is billed at $40 an hour in quarter-hour increments.
Will you ship my merchandise back to me?
Yes. If you include a prepaid shipping receipt or a carrier account, we’ll ship your products using those. Otherwise, we can invoice shipping in the final bill. We return products in the original boxes where possible.
Is white the only background color?
We can do any color or texture. Be sure to contact us with your specific requests.
How can you offer great photo for such a low price?
With over 20,000 square feet of shooting space, we’re able to consistently deliver the best product photo very efficiently. All our capacity allows us to fulfill any order, from a few photos to a complete catalog, in a matter of days. And our process works—we’ve been suppling retailers with high-quality photography for over thirty years. Start your order here.
